#### Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you access our website and interact with our content about Internet of Things and Artificial Intelligence. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

#### Collection of Personal Data

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with our website:

- **Identification data:** name and email address, when voluntarily provided through contact forms or newsletter subscriptions.
- **Browsing data:** IP address, browser type, pages accessed, time spent, and cookie data, automatically collected for analysis and improvement of the user experience.
- **Interaction data:** comments, messages, and feedback sent through the website or social media.

#### Purpose of Data Use

The personal data collected is used for the following purposes:

1. Respond to contact requests and user inquiries.
2. Send newsletters and updates about new content, with prior consent.
3. Analyze website traffic to improve content quality and browsing experience.
4. Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

#### Protection of Personal Data

We adopt appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or destruction. Among the measures adopted are:

- Use of HTTPS protocol for secure communication.
- Restriction of access to personal data only to authorized persons.
- Periodic review of our security practices.

#### Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve the browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand how you use the website. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect some website features.

#### Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

- When necessary to comply with legal obligations.
- With service providers who assist us in operating the website (such as hosting and analytics services), who are subject to confidentiality obligations.

#### Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

1. **Access:** request information about which personal data we hold about you.
2. **Correction:** request the correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.
3. **Deletion:** request the deletion of your personal data.
4. **Revocation of consent:** withdraw your consent at any time.
5. **Portability:** request the transfer of your data to another service provider.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our contact page.

#### Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the date of the last update. We recommend that you review this policy periodically.
